## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our projects seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to us via our secure submission form:

🔗 **Submit a Report:** [INSERT YOUR FORM URL HERE]

### What to Include
Please provide as much detail as possible in the form, including:
- A clear description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Potential impact
- Proof-of-concept code or screenshots (if applicable)
- Your contact information (optional, but recommended for follow-up)

### Response Timeline
- **Acknowledgment:** We aim to acknowledge receipt.
- **Status Updates:** You will receive updates on the progress of the fix.
- **Resolution:** We will coordinate with you before publicly disclosing the vulnerability.

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will credit you in our release notes (unless you wish to remain anonymous).
